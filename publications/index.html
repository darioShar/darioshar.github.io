    Dario Shariatian, Alain Durmus, and Stefano Peluchetti

    Bit-Level Discrete Diffusion with Markov Probabilistic Models: An Improved Framework with Sharp Convergence Bounds under Minimal Assumptions
    Dario Shariatian, Le-Tuyet-Nhi Pham, Antonio Ocello, and 2 more authors
    In International Conference on Machine Learning, Feb 2025
    Abs arXiv Bib Code
